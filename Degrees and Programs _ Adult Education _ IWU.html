Academic Programs

Indiana Wesleyan University offers more than 90 degree programs in several areas of study, including theology and ministry graduate programs at Wesley Seminary. Many of our programs are available in a flexible learning format, meaning you can choose to earn your degree fully online or onsite in a regional education center located in Indiana, Kentucky, or Ohio.

You can turn your full schedule into a fulfilling life. Complete your degree here from start to finish with our associate, master’s, and doctoral degree programs, or transfer your credits here from another institution with our generous transfer policy to complete your bachelor’s degree. Wherever you are, we have a degree path for you.

Continuing Education in Indiana

Indiana Wesleyan offers a variety of accelerated degree options and certificate programs to equip you to change the world in which you live. We offer programs in three key areas:

Business

The IWU DeVoe School of Business offers dynamic programs that build on the knowledge our students bring to the classroom and help shape them into lifelong learners.

Educational Leadership

Indiana Wesleyan University's School of Educational Leadership is a recognized leader in providing education programs that address the spiritual and professional challenges of P-12 educators, leaders and counselors by providing them with an integrated experience of character, scholarship and leadership development.

Service and Leadership

The IWU School of Service and Leadership offers a variety of adult education programs to help you meet your own personal and professional goals. We prepare students to become world changers by helping them to develop character, scholarship and leadership.
